# TriageAI — Smart Symptom Checker & Triage

**Target Users:**
- General public seeking health guidance
- Parents assessing children's symptoms
- Caregivers managing elderly or dependent family members
- People in areas with limited healthcare access
- Anyone unsure whether to visit the ER, urgent care, or stay home

**Value Proposition:**
TriageAI helps people make better decisions about their health by providing an intelligent symptom assessment tool. Users describe their symptoms through an intuitive interface, and TriageAI evaluates urgency, suggests possible conditions, and recommends clear next steps — from self-care at home to seeking emergency services. By reducing unnecessary ER visits and helping people recognize serious symptoms early, TriageAI saves time, money, and potentially lives.

**Key Differentiators:**
- Clean, non-clinical UI that doesn't intimidate users
- Body-map based symptom selection plus natural language input
- Color-coded urgency triage (Emergency / Urgent / Routine / Self-care)
- Personalized results based on health profile (age, sex, conditions, medications)
- Symptom history tracking to spot patterns over time
- Nearby care facility finder with map integration
- Completely free to use, no account required for basic checks

**Disclaimer:**
TriageAI is an informational tool and does not provide medical diagnoses. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Users should always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical concerns.
